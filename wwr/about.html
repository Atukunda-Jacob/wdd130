White Water Rafting

Our purpose is to provide safe, fun, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences for everyone. Our mission is to connect people with nature through adventure.

History

Founded in 1998, White Water Rafting started with a single raft and a big dream. For over 25 years we have guided thousands of guests down the most exciting rivers, creating memories that last a lifetime. Our certified guides and commitment to safety make us the #1 rafting company in the region.
